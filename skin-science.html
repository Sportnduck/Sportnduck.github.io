Explore Ingredient Science

Ceramides

Ceramides are lipids naturally found in the skin barrier. They help seal in moisture, protect against environmental stressors, and support overall skin resilience.

Peptides

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that help signal the skin to produce collagen and maintain firmness and elasticity.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is a powerful antioxidant that helps brighten skin tone, support collagen production, and protect against environmental damage.

Retinol

Retinol is a vitamin A derivative that helps accelerate skin cell turnover, smooth texture, and improve the appearance of fine lines.

Hyaluronic Acid

Hyaluronic acid is a humectant capable of holding large amounts of water, helping the skin appear hydrated, plump, and supple.
